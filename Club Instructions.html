Club Instructions

All members are expected to uphold a high standard of behavior while at the club. This means treating fellow members, staff, and animals with courtesy, respect, and professionalism. Any disruptive behavior, such as harassment, intimidation, or hostile actions, will not be tolerated and may result in disciplinary action.

Safety Guidelines

Please observe the following guidelines:

  1. Helmets and proper riding attire must be worn at all times when mounted.
  2. Follow the directions of the trainers and staff without exception.
  3. Report any hazards, unsafe conditions, or accidents to staff promptly.
  4. Do not ride or handle animals that are not assigned to you.

Horse and Stable Management

The horses are central to our club, and we ask that members handle them with care. Please follow these rules when dealing with horses and stables:

  1. Feeding the horses is not allowed unless specifically authorized.
  2. Clean up after your horse in the stable areas and training spaces.
  3. Avoid behaviors that could startle or stress the horses, such as loud noises.
  4. Always be gentle and considerate when handling the horses.
